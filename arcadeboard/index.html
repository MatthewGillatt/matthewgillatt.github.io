My Personal Arcade Controller

This is where I'll show photos of my project.

Coding comes naturally to me. I solve problems mathematically, and sometimes, making a program feels like I'm playing a puzzle game. In fact, I love playing "engineer" games such as SpaceChem or Infinifactory! Those aren't games where you find the solution, but ones where you make a solution. Basically, I have fun solving problems!

I've been researching independent game development as a hobby, but I'm not looking into joining that industry. I wanted to keep my games as "pet projects", while supporting myself with a "day job". I've experimented with Game Maker Studio and making a mod for a source port of the original Doom. One day, I hope to sell some games for cheap on places such as Steam, but I don't plan on making a living off of it. My games will be designed how I love them, not as whatever sells the best.

I'm not picky about where I want to work. It could be something simple that lets me think about my home projects, or programming something useful so I can look forward to programming something fun at home. As long as it pays the bills, you'll have my skills!
